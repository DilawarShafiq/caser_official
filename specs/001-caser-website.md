# Specification: CASER Website V1

## 1. Overview
Create a professional, high-impact website for CASER. The site must serve as a hub for research, data analytics, and policy advisory.

## 2. Sitemap
### Header Navigation
- Home
- About Us
- What We Do
- Focus Areas
- Research and Publications
- What’s Up

### Footer
- Quick Links
- Social Media Icons
- Newsletter Signup
- Contact Info

### Hierarchy
- **Home**
- **About Us**
    - Who We Are
    - Our Team
    - Partners & Collaborators
- **What We Do / Services**
    - Data Analytics & Visualization
    - Surveys & Field Research
    - Policy Advisory
    - Capacity Building & Training
    - Impact Evaluation
- **Themes / Focus Areas**
    - Health & Wellbeing
    - Education & Skills

    - Reports

    - News & Announcements
    - Events
- **Careers** (Footer Link)
    - Current Openings

- **Contact & Connect** (Footer Link)
    - Contact Form
    - Office Location & Map

## 3. Homepage Content
- **Headline:** “Building the Future of Evidence-Based Research in Pakistan”
- **Subtext:** “A new initiative dedicated to high-quality, data-driven insights for policy, business, and society. Our first projects are on the way.”
- **Buttons:** “Learn More About Us” (Primary), “Join Our Network” (Secondary).

## 4. Visual Guidelines
- **Typography:**
    - Headings: Montserrat (Bold for H1, Medium for H2/H3).
    - Body: Open Sans (Regular).
- **UI Elements:**
    - Buttons: Rounded corners, primary color fill, hover effects.
    - Icons: Minimalist line icons, golden accent.
    - Layout: Grid-based, ample whitespace.
